POINT BLANK Logic.™ is one software engineer lead (MS in Computer Science with 25+ years of experience) who occasionally teams up with other skilled professionals (in engineering, marketing, sales, legal, etc...) depending on the project.

We work remotely.
Southern California and the South of France are our typical hangout locations. In other words, the time zones we tend to operate from are: UTC - 8 (US West Coast time) and UTC + 1 (France time).

We work remotely because the Internet and the computing capabilities of our world has enabled it for years already. Over this time, at our level we have contributed to the spread of these Internet tools that mitigate geographical constraints, and have simply been walking the talk ever since.

Consequently, we are readily available for clients wherever their business happens to be located. This is the sensible way to work in this day and age: minimal commute with maximal geographical reach.
By the way we have been saying that for years! Long before 2020, when suddenly, people woke up to it...

So much time, energy, stress and money has been saved already with this approach when we gladly stopped dealing with the irresponsible daily insanity that is rush hour traffic.
