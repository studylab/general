We find solutions to your computing needs.

We are software engineers. We do software developments, consulting in software engineering and applied Computer Science, and occasionally computer operating services (sysadmin, dba, devops).

We work 99% remotely, usually from the US west coast time zone. We remain open to visit a client when it can be a productive meeting.

Working 99% remotely allows us to be available to our clients anywhere their company happens to be located. We are immediately operational to consider and work on your project, with any department of your company. There is no time/money wasted on physical world constraints: commuting of course, all kinds of interruptions unrelated to the advancement of the project, setting up an office space, just to list a few.

One important aspect of our work is that we favor free and open source software (FOSS) in our solutions. That way you have access to the entire code base. As an important consequence, our clients should not be trapped into a proprietary technology.
